East of downtown. North of extraordinary.

The Oxley is connected to Commercial Drive, one of the most engaging and energetic communities in the city. This neighbourhood has emerged as a hive of change, and The Oxley is poised as another catalyst in its evolution. The area is home to some of the best Vancouver has to offer and a gathering place for neighbors: coffee shops and craft breweries, parks, schools and top rated restaurants, not to mention galleries, book shops and furniture stores.

Just a few blocks away from The Oxley are the charming districts of Strathcona and Hastings Sunrise where exciting new amenities are popping up at a fierce pace.
